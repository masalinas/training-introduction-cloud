  • Service 
    The service groups sets of pods together and provides a way to refer to them from a single static IP address and the corresponding DNS name. It can reference a single pod, a group of individual pods, or pods managed by ReplicaSets or Deployments. Below, we provide a sample service:
    • 
apiVersion: v1
kind: Service
metadata:
    name: frontend
    labels:
    app: dockchat
    tier: frontend
spec:
    type: LoadBalancer
    ports:
    - port: 9500
        targetPort: 5000
    selector:
    app: dockchat
    tier: frontend
